Voluntary Donation Links


  1. The Hemkunt Foundation

    Hemkunt Foundation is a non-government organisation that aims to provide humanitarian aid to marginalized sections of society. The organisation is based upon the most popular Sikh principle of ‘Sarbat Da Bhalla’ which means welfare for all. While the efforts of Hemkunt Foundation are in tandem with its principle, it continues to provide livelihoods, disaster relief, access to basic human rights and education to all.

  2. Paytm Foundation

    Paytm Foundation is raising ₹14 crore to donate Oxygen Concentrators across India. Paytm will match the contributions received through this initiative.

  3. Zomato Feeding India

    Feeding India by Zomato is a 100% not for profit organisation, every rupee you donate will go towards supporting the hospitals and patients in need.

  4. ACT Grants

    ACT Grants are centrally distributing oxygen on behalf of the entire Indian startup ecosystem and are also partnering with the government to support their efforts in this crisis. International payments are accepted.

  5. Goonj

    Goonj are working in parts of 27 States/UTs of India with their teams and an ongoing network of 400+ grassroots organizations. They focus and engage with ignored communities such as artisans, transgenders, sex workers, patients etc.
